Attack on Titans

Full Description

Humanity fights back against giant man-eating Titans in a brutal and suspenseful war for survival. Set in a world where humanity has been pushed to the brink of extinction by these Titans, the story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the elite Survey Corps to uncover the truth behind the Titans and to fight for their survival.
