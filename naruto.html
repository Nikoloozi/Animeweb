Naruto

Full Description

Naruto Uzumaki, a spirited young ninja with a sealed demon fox inside him, dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and earning the respect of his village. As he trains and grows stronger, Naruto forms bonds with his teammates, faces powerful enemies, and uncovers the truth behind his mysterious past in this action-packed and emotional journey.
